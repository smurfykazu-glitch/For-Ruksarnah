HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Happy birthday, Ruksarnah

I still remember the first time we met when we were little. And now, you’ve grown into a young, mature woman.

As you step into this new chapter of your life, I pray that Allah guides you, protects you, and fills your days with happiness, success, and endless blessings.

May this day be the beginning of a lifetime filled with smiles… and may all your dreams come true.

For more than five years, even when we were out of touch, you never really left my thoughts. Last April, when I finally found your social accounts, I felt happier than I can properly explain. And when we started texting… I know I might have annoyed you, always asking if you were online. I’m sorry for that. I’m also sorry if my messages ever felt dry. The truth is, I get nervous talking to you.

I get even more nervous when I see you in person. That’s why, when I come to your store, I end up facing your mom instead of you. But honestly… I was stealing glances at you the whole time. I hope that didn’t make you uncomfortable.

It’s just that you look even more beautiful in real life than in photos. When you smile, it feels like everything else fades. When you giggle, the world slows down for a moment. When you speak, your voice has this calmness that stays with me. And more than anything, it’s your personality that amazes me. I’ve never met someone quite like you.

Somewhere along the way, without me even realizing it, you became someone really special to me. I don’t think I ever truly moved on from you… maybe I never wanted to.

I truly admire who you are, and I hope your life is filled with happiness, always.

I spent hours making this website and writing this letter for you. There’s still so much more I want to say… but I’ll stop here for now.

May Allah answer every prayer you’ve ever made, protect your heart, fill your life with joy, and guide your steps toward all that you hope for.

With all my prayers and best wishes,
Mustafa

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ أَيَّامَكِ مَلِيئَةً بِالْفَرَحِ وَالرِّضَا، وَاحْفَظْ قَلْبَكِ وَنَفْسَكِ، وَبَارِكْ لَكِ فِي كُلِّ خُطْوَةٍ تَخْطُوهَا.

May Allah fill your days with joy and contentment, protect your heart and soul, and bless every step you take.
